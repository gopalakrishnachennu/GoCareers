📖 Metric Definitions (click to expand)
Jobs — All-time RawJob rows for this platform.
Synced% — % of jobs pushed to the vet pool (sync_status=SYNCED).
Pending% — % of jobs waiting in PENDING sync. High = backlog or gate rejections.
Runs — CompanyFetchRun records in the selected window.
Success% — (SUCCESS + 0.5×PARTIAL) / total runs. Measures harvest reliability.
ZeroYield% — % of runs that fetched 0 jobs (status=EMPTY). High = companies have no open roles, or board is broken.
Fail% — % of runs that failed with an error (timeout, HTTP 4xx/5xx, parse error).
Inactive% — % of harvested jobs marked is_active=False (closed / expired).
Missing JD% — % of jobs with no description text (has_description=False). High = detail fetch broken or source withholds JD.
Req% / Resp% — % of jobs with requirements / responsibilities fields populated. Source capability shown in parentheses.
Salary% — % of jobs with salary_min or salary_max populated.
Dept% — % of jobs with a non-empty department field.
Risk — Composite score 0–100: (empty_rate×25) + (fail_rate×35) + (parse_error_rate×25) + (blocked_rate×15). Shown only when ≥{{ min_runs_for_risk }} runs exist — marked "–" otherwise.
Source tier — api=public REST/GraphQL, semi=unofficial JSON, scrape=HTML only.
Gap badges — N/A source doesn't provide this field. ⚠ parser source has it but we're not extracting it (parser gap).
