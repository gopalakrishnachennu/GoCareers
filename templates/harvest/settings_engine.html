📊 How to read the numbers

Full scan estimate

Total companies ÷ (rate × concurrency) = minutes.
e.g. 967 companies ÷ ({{ cfg.task_rate_limit }} × {{ cfg.worker_concurrency }}) = ~{{ cfg.worker_concurrency|add:0 }} min

CPU safe zone (CX23)

Keep concurrency ≤ {{ recommended_concurrency }} and rate ≤ 8/min to stay under 80% CPU. Above that, the app starts timing out for users.

Rate limit vs stagger

Stagger controls how fast tasks are queued. Rate limit controls how fast workers execute. Both together = smooth, predictable throughput.
